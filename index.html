Ridgeback Dogs

This webpage is all about Ridgeback dogs

Fun Facts

  • Cat Lovers: They love cats!
  • Chasers: They love chasing prey!
  • Bottomless Pits: They will not stop eating!
  • Smart but Reckless

Bred to hunt lions

These hounds were able to keep a lion occupied long enough for their masters to make the kill in order to protect their village and home. Because Ridgebacks were bred to protect their families from the many dangers lurking in the shadows of the African terrain, their senses are as keen as they come.
